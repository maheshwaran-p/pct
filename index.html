history

Since 2001, Powerchip Technologies India Pvt ltd (PCT) has been committed to providing high quality security system solutions and most importantly, personalized service to its residential, commercial & Industrial Customers.

With over 1,500 professional installations, PCT has necessary expertise and resources available to design, install, and maintain customized solutions for protecting families, homes, and businesses.

Mission

PCT offers a wide array of security products including complete security systems solutions, including CCTV, video monitoring, access control, fire detection, perimeter boundary sensors and more. In addition, we are experts in fully integrated solutions for residential and business applications.

At PCT, we understand the importance of installing quality products and providing exemplary customer service. That is precisely why we are a leader in security system installations in South India and have a proven track record of complete Customer satisfaction.

Collective experience and exposure to various business streams and operations is the core strength of the PCT Management team. This solid foundation is the right pedestal for many tall achievements in the years to come.

Our basic policy

Provide customers with after-sales service and information to maximize their security & comfort level. Employ personnel with solid skills to ensure top-quality maintenance and ensure that customers can enjoy the experience of our quality service with peace of mind
